
# JIET Universe Jodhpur - Complete Master Document

---

## *Institution Overview*

**JIET (Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology)** is an esteemed and prestigious institution of Engineering and Management located in Rajasthan, India. The institute is dedicated to promoting technical and scientific literacy and temperament among young inquisitive minds.

- **Establishment Date:** Established in 2003
- **Location:** Western Rajasthan (Jodhpur)
- **Type:** Private Autonomous Institute
- **Scope:** Multi-disciplinary educational institution offering courses across multiple domains

### Mission and Vision
JIET is devoted to providing quality education and implementation processes with a focus on:
- Quality education with practical emphasis
- Latest teaching methodologies and technology integration
- Industry-aligned curriculum
- Development of skilled professionals for global workforce
- Research and innovation

---

## *JIET Universe (Complete Institutional Group)*

JIET is not just an engineering college but part of the JIET Universe, a comprehensive educational and healthcare group that includes:

- **Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology** - Engineering, M.Tech, and PhD programs
- **JIET Group of Institutions** - Multiple schools including Design, Management, and Computer Applications
- **Jodhpur Institute of Hotel Management (JIHM)** - Hospitality and Hotel Management education (affiliated with NCHMCT, New Delhi)
- **Jodhpur Institute of Design and Technology** - Design programs
- **JIET College of Nursing** - Nursing and healthcare programs
- **JIET Hospital and Cancer Research Center** - Healthcare and research facility
- **BCA and MCA Programs** - Computer applications courses

## *History and Accreditations*

### Founding and Recognition
- **Established:** 2003
- **Approved by:** AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education), New Delhi
- **Affiliated to:** Bikaner Technical University (BTU), Rajasthan
- **Autonomous Status:** Granted by UGC for 10 years (2020-2021 to 2029-2030)
- JIET is the **first engineering college of Rajasthan** to achieve autonomous status

### Accreditations and Certifications
- **NAAC Accreditation:** Grade 'A' - The only engineering college of Rajasthan with NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation
- **NBA Accreditation:**
  - Accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi
  - First engineering institution in Western Rajasthan with NBA accreditation in four UG programs since 2011
  - Received NBA accreditation three times consecutively
- **Academic Excellence:**
  - Ranked 1st among all BTU-affiliated colleges for three consecutive years (2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21)

## *Undergraduate Programs -- B.Tech*

### Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) - 4 Years Full-Time

**Course Specializations:**
- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- CSE with AI/ML (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)
- CSE with Cyber Security
- Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
- Electrical Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering
- Civil Engineering
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Data Science
- Cloud Computing

### Eligibility Criteria for B.Tech
- **Education:** Completion of 12th grade or equivalent
- **Minimum Percentage:** 45-50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM)
- **Admission Process:** JEE Main scores (preferred), REAP scores accepted, Merit-based admission, Direct admission option available
- **Minimum cutoff:** ~60 percentile in JEE Main

### Program Structure
- **Choice Based Credit System:** Latest CBCS implementation
- **Curriculum:** Industry-aligned and created by field veterans
- **Practical Learning:** Emphasis on hands-on experience
- **Duration:** 4 years (8 semesters)
